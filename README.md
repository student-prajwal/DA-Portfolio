### Project 3: E-Commerce Sales & Profitability Analysis (Phase 2 Capstone)
**Business Question:** What drives revenue and profitability across categories, regions, and customer segments?

**Approach:** End-to-end analysis using SQL for data extraction, Python for EDA and visualization, and Tableau for interactive dashboarding. Delivered findings through a 5-slide business presentation.

**Tools:** Python, SQL, Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn, Tableau

**Key Finding:** 34.4% of orders are loss-making despite Clothing being the top revenue category — indicating a pricing/cost issue rather than a discounting problem.

**Notebook:** `Phase2_Capstone_Plan.ipynb`

**Presentation:** `E-Commerce_Sales___Profitability_Analysis.pptx`

**Dashboard:** [View Dashboard](https://public.tableau.com/app/profile/prajwal.nh/viz/dashboard_v2025_3/EcommerceCapstoneDashboard)
